ServerSide.swift

Server-Side Swift Conference

30th October - 1st November 2019. Copenhagen, Denmark.

Become Sponsor Buy a Ticket

The Conference

ServerSide.swift is a framework-independent conference, where we will learn and share on a number of different related topics. The conference is aimed at being a non-profit conference and solely run for the love of server-side Swift.

Where and When

The conference will take place in awesome Copenhagen, Denmark and last for 3 days from 30th Oct - 1st Nov 2019! October 30th will be a day of workshops and October 31th - November 1st will be packed with inspiring talks.

Speakers

We have an awesome line up of speakers with some incredible talks. Check out our Speakers page to see them all! Please note that our CFP is open: register a talk here.

The Team

Collaboration between Martin J. Lasek (Berlin - DE), Tim Condon (Manchester - UK), Steffen D. Sommer (Copenhagen - DK)

Sponsoring

We are currently looking for sponsors so if you work for a company that would want to help sponsor ServerSide.swift then please get in contact on sponsors@serversideswift.info

Want to help?

As we are a non-profit conference then the best way you can help at the moment is by spreading the word! Feel free to share the link on whatever platforms that you like.
