ServerSide.swift

Server-Side Swift Conference

9th December 2022. London, UK

Become a SponsorBuy a Ticket

The Conference

ServerSide.swift is a framework-independent conference, where we will learn and share on a number of different related topics. The conference is aimed at being a non-profit conference and solely run for the love of server-side Swift.

Where and When

The conference will take place on the 9th of December in the awesome London, UK, and it will be packed with inspiring talks.

Speakers

We have a packed schedule full of amazing talks from some amazing speakers. Take a look!

The Team

The conference is organised by Tim Condon and helped by a number of volunteers. If you'd like to volunteer to help out at the conference, please get in touch!

Sponsoring

We are currently looking for sponsors so if you work for a company that would want to help sponsor ServerSide.swift then please get in contact at sponsors@serversideswift.info.

Want to help?

As we are a non-profit conference then the best way you can help at the moment is by spreading the word! Feel free to share the link on whatever platforms that you like. If you'd like to volunteer at the conference then please get in touch!

Newsletter

The best way to keep posted about the conference is by signing up below! Future newsletters will contain everything about sponsor levels, ticket prices, schedule and more!
