Location

In the heart of London

The Venue

The conference wiil take place in the heart of London in Kensington.

We have an amazing location with the main conference taking place in an IMAX theatre.

Please note that due to venue restictions the specific details for the venue will be sent out via email.

Transportation

The best way to get to the conference is to use the London Underground. The venue is a short walk away from South Kensington and Gloucester Road, which are on the Circle, District and Piccadilly lines.

The venue is also well served by London Buses, and any bus route that goes to South Kensington is a short route away.

Please note - there is no parking at the venue. We strongly recommend you use public transport.

Hotels

There are many hotels around London to suit all budgets and any hotels that allow you to get to South Kensington or Gloucester Road Underground stations easily will work

Some good options are the Best Western Mornington or the Millenium Hotel London Knightsbridge with many other hotels nearby.

Alternatively, check out Dorsett Shepherds Bush London or Novotell London West which are a short tube journey away from the venue.
